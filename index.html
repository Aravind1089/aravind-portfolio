My Journey

I was born and brought up in southern Tamil Nadu and moved to Chennai in 2013 to kickstart my career in data. My journey began with MS Access development where I also gained hands-on experience in SQL, VBA, and Excel macros. These early days helped me automate repetitive tasks.

I then transitioned to QlikView development, leading migration from Pentaho dashboards. I gained deeper understanding of enterprise data environments and PostgreSQL integration.

A major milestone was working with an MIS team where I implemented a Java-based tool and Excel automation to generate PowerPoint decks for 32 countries in 15 minutes, saving 4.5 FTEs.

At Sutherland, I mastered Power BI, building enterprise dashboards. At Accenture, I automated over 250 reports across 12+ global contracts, led a team, and delivered data-driven decisions using Power Apps and Power Automate.
